Mental Health Resources

Prioritizing Your Well-Being

Maintaining your mental health is just as important as academic success. The University of Michigan provides numerous services through Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS).

  • Individual & Group Counseling: Speak with a licensed therapist to discuss concerns and develop coping strategies.
  • Workshops & Drop-In Sessions: Participate in guided discussions and mindfulness exercises.
  • Crisis Support: Emergency services are available for students in urgent need of help.

For more details, visit CAPS.
