Academic Support at UMSI

Academic Advising

The Academic Programs and Student Life (APSL) team at the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI) is dedicated to supporting students' academic success. The APSL team offers academic advising for UMSI students in the Bachelor of Science in Information (BSI), Master of Applied Data Science (MADS), Master of Health Informatics (MHI), and Master of Science in Information (MSI) programs. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Students can schedule appointments with academic advisors to receive personalized guidance on course selection, degree requirements, and academic planning. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Additional Academic Resources

  • University Library System: Access extensive digital and physical collections essential for research and coursework.
  • Writing Center: Enhance your writing skills through workshops and one-on-one consultations.
  • Study Spaces: Utilize designated study areas across campus to support your learning environment.
