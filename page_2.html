UMSI Tutoring Services

Get Support with Your Courses

UMSI provides tutoring services to help students excel in their coursework. Whether you need help understanding complex topics, preparing for exams, or improving study strategies, tutoring is available.

  • One-on-One Tutoring: Get personalized assistance tailored to your academic needs.
  • Group Study Sessions: Learn collaboratively with peers on similar coursework.
  • Workshops & Study Skills: Attend sessions focused on time management, exam strategies, and more.

For more details, visit the UMSI Tutoring website.
